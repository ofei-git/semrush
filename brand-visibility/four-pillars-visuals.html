Four Pillars of Brand Visibility — Visual Concepts

Six approaches to visualising the framework. Pick the one that teaches best.

Concept 1: EEAT Venn

Mirrors Google's EEAT diagram. Trust at the center where all three overlap. Familiar to the SEO audience.

Brand Visibility
Discoverability Can they find you?
Clarity Do they understand you?
Authority Do others validate you?
Trust The keystone

Concept 2: Temple

Discoverability is the foundation. Clarity and Authority are the pillars. Trust is the capstone. Brand Visibility is the roof.

The Four Pillars of Brand Visibility
Brand Visibility The outcome
Brand Trust Will AI confidently recommend or act on your brand? Keystone
Brand Clarity Do they understand you correctly?
Brand Authority Do others validate your position?
Brand Discoverability Can AI systems find and retrieve your content? Foundation

Concept 3: Concentric Rings

Trust at the center, each ring represents a wider sphere of brand signals. Communicates that Trust is what everything converges toward.

Brand Visibility
Trust
Discoverability The foundation Clarity Understood correctly Authority Validated by others ↓ ↓ ↓ Every ring converges toward Trust

Concept 4: Table

Four legs hold up Brand Visibility. Trust is the strongest leg — remove it and everything tips. Simple, literal, effective.

The Four Pillars of Brand Visibility
Brand Visibility The outcome all four pillars support
Discover-ability Find you
Clarity Understand you
Authority Validate you
Trust Keystone

Remove any leg and the surface wobbles. Remove Trust and it collapses.
