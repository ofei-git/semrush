What's Already Happening

This isn't a prediction piece. These are products shipping, protocols being adopted, and behaviors changing right now.

Feb 2025

Deep research launches in ChatGPT

The first time most people experienced AI that investigates independently across dozens of sources and reports back. Gemini and Perplexity follow within months.

Jun 2025

Anthropic ships Claude Code

AI that writes, tests, debugs, and commits code autonomously across entire projects. One of the first widely-used agentic developer tools.

Jul 2025

Perplexity launches Comet browser

Search-first agentic browsing. By September, Comet drives 52.5% of all agentic web traffic, overtaking ChatGPT's Atlas.

Oct 2025

OpenAI launches Atlas with Agent Mode

Autonomously executes multi-step tasks — finding flights, comparing prices, completing purchases inside the browser.

Nov 2025

OpenClaw goes viral

149K GitHub stars in one week. 770K autonomous agents deployed. A run on Mac Minis. People building personal AI agents that manage finances, homeschooling, grocery ordering, and business operations.

Dec 2025

MCP donated to Linux Foundation

Microsoft, Google, Anthropic, and OpenAI form the Agentic AI Foundation to develop open-source agent standards. MCP becomes the dominant protocol with 97M+ monthly SDK downloads.

Jan 2026

Google launches Chrome auto-browse and publishes SAGE

Gemini-powered autonomous browsing ships to Chrome. SAGE research paper trains agents that chain 4.9 search steps per query. Google also launches Universal Commerce Protocol for agent-to-merchant transactions.

Mar 2026

Bot traffic surpasses human traffic

Automated traffic hits 51% of all web traffic. AI bot visits jump from 1:200 human visits (Q1 2025) to 1:31 (Q4 2025). CTR from AI platforms to publishers collapses to 0.27%.
