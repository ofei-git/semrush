Strategy in one sentence Own the "sustainable homeware" buying journey through product page conversion and off-site brand authority — not by outpublishing bigger competitors on content volume.

What business outcome is SEO serving?

Grow organic revenue 50% in 18 months. Reduce paid ad dependency from 65% to under 45%.
SEO's Role
Improve product page conversion and build brand authority across buying guides, communities, and AI recommendations.

2
Audience
Who are you reaching and where do they look?
Who
Sustainability-conscious consumers, 28-45, researching homeware purchases. Care about materials, sourcing, durability.
Where They Search
Google — product + comparison queries Primary
Reddit — brand recs, community trust High
AI tools — comparative research Growing
Instagram / Pinterest — visual discovery Low
Key Insight
Post-purchase surveys show customers compare 3-4 brands before buying. We need to be present at each comparison point.
3
Strategic Focus
What are you investing in vs. deliberately skipping?
Investing In
Product + category page overhaul (all ~20 core pages)
10-12 buying guides targeting mid-funnel research queries
Off-site authority: gift guides, product seeding, review generation (15-20 pitches/month)
Community presence: Reddit + sustainability forums (5+ hrs/week)
Deliberately Skipping
Blog scaling / high-volume informational content
Video and podcast production (Year 2 consideration)
Traditional link building campaigns (investing in mentions and citations instead)
Broad informational terms ("what is sustainable living")
Topics We're Owning
Sustainable bedding (materials, certifications) · Eco-friendly kitchen & dining · Transparent pricing & sourcing
4
Authority Play
What kind of authority are you building?
Content Product authority through transparency — materials sourcing, certifications, honest pricing on every page.
Off-site 15-20 outreach pitches/month. 4 seasonal gift guide campaigns. Product seeding for reviews. Unlinked mention monitoring. Reddit 5+ hrs/week.
Technical Mobile speed 3.8s → under 2s. Structured data on all product pages. AI crawler access.
